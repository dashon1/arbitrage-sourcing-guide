The Problem

Why Most People Fail at Retail Arbitrage

Sourcing profitable products sounds simple, but the details matter. Here's what's holding most people back.

💸

Buying Without Research

They see a deal, get excited, and buy without checking actual resale values. Then they're stuck with items they can't sell at a profit.

📊

Ignoring the Math

They price items based on current listings instead of sold prices, then wonder why their profit disappears after platform fees.

🎯

No Systematic Approach

They hop between platforms, waste hours on bad deals, and never build a repeatable system for finding profitable inventory.

🤷

Negotiation Fails

They either pay full price or scare off sellers with ridiculous lowballs. They never learn the psychology of closing deals.
