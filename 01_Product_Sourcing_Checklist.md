## Pre-Sourcing Preparation

### Before You Leave Home

- [ ] **Define your target categories** for today's sourcing trip
- [ ] **Check weather forecast** - outdoor sales cancelled in rain
- [ ] **Review your budget** - know your max spend per item
- [ ] **Calculate fuel/transport costs** - factor into margin calculations
- [ ] **Charge your phone** - essential for checking online prices
- [ ] **Update your pricing app** - have eBay/Sold same-day ready
- [ ] **Prepare cash + cards** - some sellers don't accept cards
- [ ] **Check platform alerts** - new listings in your target categories
- [ ] **Map your route** - group nearby pickups efficiently
- [ ] **Set time limits** - don't over-spend time hunting

### What to Bring

- [ ] Measuring tape
- [ ] Flashlight (for basement/storage sales)
- [ ] Packing blankets
- [ ] Hand truck or cart
- [ ] Ziploc bags for small parts
- [ ] Sharpie markers
- [ ] Cleaning wipes
- [ ] Receipt book or invoice template
- [ ] Phone charger
- [ ] Business cards

## Platform-Specific Search Strategies

### Facebook Marketplace

- [ ] Search by category + location radius (25 miles)
- [ ] Use price filters: $5-50 for beginners
- [ ] Filter by "within 10 miles" for pickups
- [ ] Search for "must sell", "moving", "downsizing"
- [ ] Check " Offers" section for motivated sellers
- [ ] Look for estate sale and estate liquidation keywords
- [ ] Save searches for daily email alerts
- [ ] Check marketplace during morning hours (7-9 AM)
- [ ] Respond within 1 hour for best deals

**Priority Keywords:**
- "clear out"
- "moved"
- "divorce sale"
- "estate"
- "relocating"
- "kids outgrowing"
- "redecorating"
- "renovation"

### Craigslist

- [ ] Search both "For Sale" and "Free" sections
- [ ] Use the "byseller" option to avoid dealers
- [ ] Check "sold" listings to see what moves fast
- [ ] Look for posts with photos (serious sellers)
- [ ] Filter by "listing date" for recent posts
- [ ] Use the flag feature to filter scrapers
- [ ] Check "garage sale" + neighborhood names

**Best Search Terms:**
- "garage sale"
- "estate sale"
- "family yard sale"
- "multi-family"
- "baby stuff"
- "furniture"

### OfferUp / LetGo

- [ ] Enable location services for nearby listings
- [ ] Use the "Make an Offer" filter
- [ ] Check "Urgent" section for price drops
- [ ] Look for bundles at 50%+ off
- [ ] Message during off-peak hours
- [ ] Verify seller ratings before meeting

### Thrift Stores & Consignment

- [ ] Visit on restock days (often Tuesdays/Thursdays)
- [ ] Check color-tag sales (often 50% off tags)
- [ ] Look for senior/discount days
- [ ] Build relationships with staff
- [ ] Know your store's return policies
- [ ] Bring a cart - items go fast
- [ ] Check electronics section first thing

## Product Evaluation Criteria

### Must-Have Criteria (ALL must pass)

- [ ] **Verified sold listings exist** - minimum 3 in past 30 days
- [ ] **Current competitive prices** - checked within 24 hours
- [ ] **Clear understanding of condition** - no hidden damage
- [ ] **Profitable after all fees** - minimum 30% ROI
- [ ] **Easy to list** - clear photos possible
- [ ] **Compact storage** - can you store it?
- [ ] **Legal to resell** - no trademark issues
- [ ] **Authentic** - verified genuine product

### Secondary Evaluation Factors

- [ ] **Shipping friendly** - under 5 lbs
- [ ] **Non-breakable** - no special packaging
- [ ] **Not季节性** - consistent year-round demand
- [ ] **Clear branding** - easy to identify
- [ ] **Standard sizing** - no fit issues
- [ ] **Complete set** - all pieces included
- [ ] **Original packaging** - commands premium
- [ ] **NotHazmat** - no special shipping required

### Red Flags That Kill Deals

- [ ] Multiple identical items (scam risk)
- [ ] "One owner" luxury items (stolen goods risk)
- [ ] Items requiring professional installation
- [ ] Products with serial number tracking
- [ ] Items missing key components
- [ ] Counterfeit risk categories (luxury bags, electronics)
- [ ] Anything requiring special licenses
- [ ] Perishables or items with expiration dates

## Profit Margin Calculation

### The 30-Second Test

For every potential purchase, calculate:

```
(Selling Price - Fees - Shipping) - (Purchase Price + Gas + Time)
= YOUR PROFIT
```

**Minimum Acceptable Profit:** $15 per item (for small items)
**Target Profit:** $30+ per item (for medium items)
**Premium Target:** $50+ per item (for large items)

### Break-Even Calculator

**Before you buy, verify:**

- [ ] eBay final value fee: 13.25% (roughly)
- [ ] PayPal fee: 2.9% + $0.30
- [ ] Shipping estimate: weight-based calculator
- [ ] Packing materials: $1-3 per box
- [ ] Your time value: $20/hour minimum
- [ ] Travel costs: $0.50/mile

**Formula for Minimum Selling Price:**

```
Purchase Price ÷ 0.65 = Break-Even Price
Break-Even Price × 1.5 = Target Selling Price
```

### Condition-Based Multipliers

| Condition | Multiplier | Notes |
|-----------|------------|-------|
| New/Open Box | 85-100% | Original packaging intact |
| Like New | 70-85% | Minimal use, no damage |
| Very Good | 55-70% | Light wear, functional |
| Good | 40-55% | Visible wear, fully functional |
| Acceptable | 25-40% | Heavy wear, complete |

## Authentication Verification Steps

### Electronics

- [ ] Check serial numbers against database
- [ ] Verify model numbers match official specs
- [ ] Test all functions personally
- [ ] Check for water damage indicators
- [ ] Verify accessories are genuine
- [ ] Search serial on stolen databases (high-value items)

### Luxury Items

- [ ] Research authenticating on dedicated forums
- [ ] Check serial numbers against manufacturer
- [ ] Verify materials and construction
- [ ] Compare with verified authentic photos
- [ ] When in doubt, walk away

### Collectibles

- [ ] Verify edition/print run numbers
- [ ] Check condition grading standards
- [ ] Research market demand on dedicated forums
- [ ] Confirm all pieces/parts present
- [ ] Look for restoration/refinishing indicators

## Negotiation Tactics

### Opening Moves

- [ ] **Don't show excitement** - act indifferent
- [ ] **Start 30-40% below asking** - room to negotiate up
- [ ] **Use time pressure** - "I need to decide today"
- [ ] **Offer cash** - immediate payment has value
- [ ] **Bundle multiple items** - increases your leverage
- [ ] **Be ready to walk** - the best deals come back

### Counter-Offer Responses

- [ ] "I can do $X, but that's my final offer"
- [ ] "I'll take all of it for $X" (bundle tactic)
- [ ] "I can pick it up today with cash"
- [ ] "I've seen similar for $X, that's my budget"
- [ ] "Can you do better?" (classic opener)

### Closing Strategies

- [ ] Have exact cash ready to show seriousness
- [ ] Offer to help load/move (added value)
- [ ] Give them a reason to prefer you ("I'm local")
- [ ] Be prepared to pay more for convenience
- [ ] Know your absolute maximum before negotiating

## Condition Assessment Guide

### New (N) - Never Used

- [ ] Tags still attached or original packaging sealed
- [ ] No signs of handling
- [ ] Perfect in every way
- [ ] Can command full retail price

### Open Box (OB) - Like New

- [ ] Original packaging opened
- [ ] Item inspected and confirmed working
- [ ] No cosmetic damage
- [ ] All accessories included
- [ ] Typically 85-95% of retail

### Like New (LN)

- [ ] Used 1-3 times
- [ ] No visible wear
- [ ] Fully functional
- [ ] Clean and well-maintained
- [ ] Typically 70-85% of retail

### Very Good (VG)

- [ ] Some signs of use
- [ ] No damage affecting function
- [ ] Minor cosmetic wear
- [ ] Clean and presentable
- [ ] Typically 55-70% of retail

### Good (G)

- [ ] Normal signs of use
- [ ] Fully functional
- [ ] Minor cosmetic damage
- [ ] May need minor cleaning
- [ ] Typically 40-55% of retail

### Acceptable (A)

- [ ] Heavy wear
- [ ] Still functional
- [ ] Visible damage
- [ ] Works but imperfect
- [ ] Typically 25-40% of retail

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Research Failures

- [ ] Not checking sold prices (only current listings)
- [ ] Forgetting to account for all fees
- [ ] Ignoring competition quantity
- [ ] Missing the 90-day sales data
- [ ] Not verifying authentic resale eligibility

### Operational Mistakes

- [ ] Buying damaged goods unknowingly
- [ ] Overestimating seller's shipping abilities
- [ ] Not documenting condition with photos
- [ ] Paying before inspection complete
- [ ] Storing items before final sale confirmed

### Financial Mistakes

- [ ] Buying without calculating minimum price
- [ ] Underestimating shipping costs
- [ ] Ignoring your time value
- [ ] Buying on emotion, not math
- [ ] Not keeping transaction records

### Safety Mistakes

- [ ] Meeting strangers alone
- [ ] Going to unknown locations at night
- [ ] Carrying too much cash
- [ ] Not sharing location with someone
- [ ] Accepting unusual payment methods
