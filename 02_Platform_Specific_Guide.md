## 1. Facebook Marketplace

### Platform Overview

Facebook Marketplace is the #1 source for retail arbitrage opportunities in most US markets. With 1 billion monthly users and seamless integration with Facebook's ecosystem, it's where motivated sellers go to move inventory quickly.

### Target Demographics

- **Primary Sellers:** Families downsizing, estate executors, small business liquidators
- **Secondary Sellers:** Moving families, divorcing couples, new parents
- **Avoid:** Professional resellers (against ToS), bulk dealers

### Advanced Search Strategies

#### Search Syntax Mastery

```
"exact phrases" -exclude this
Category: "Baby & Kids" price:10..50
location:25 miles from "City Name"
Posted:today
```

#### High-Value Search Combinations

| Search Term | Success Rate | Best For |
|-------------|--------------|----------|
| "must sell" + "negotiable" | 85% | Highly motivated sellers |
| "divorce" + "everything" | 75% | Complete home contents |
| "estate" + "moved" | 80% | Quality antiques, collectibles |
| "moving" + "relocating" | 70% | Mid-range furniture, electronics |
| "kids outgrowing" | 90% | Baby gear, toys, clothing |
| "clear out" + "renovation" | 65% | Home improvement items |
| "mother's/father's estate" | 80% | Quality household items |
| "down-sizing" | 75% | Senior moving sales |

#### Optimal Posting Times

- **Best:** Tuesday-Thursday, 6-9 AM
- **Good:** Any weekday morning
- **Avoid:** Friday evening-Sunday (competition peaks)

#### Message Timing Strategy

- **Within 1 hour:** Highest response rate
- **1-3 hours:** Good response rate
- **Same day:** Acceptable
- **Next day:** Low response (someone else bought it)

### Negotiation Tactics

#### Opening Message Template

```
"Hi! I'm interested in the [ITEM NAME].
Is it still available? I can pick up today
with cash and help you load it."

[INSERT PICTURE OF YOUR CAR/BUSINESS CARD]
```

#### Counter-Offer Sequence

1. **First offer:** 25-35% below asking price
2. **Counter response:** Split the difference
3. **Final offer:** 15-20% below asking
4. **Walk away phrase:** "I can meet you at $X, that's my absolute max today"

### Safety Protocols

- [ ] Always meet in public, well-lit locations
- [ ] Consider police station parking lots
- [ ] Tell someone where you're going
- [ ] Bring your phone with 911 ready
- [ ] Trust your instincts - if it feels wrong, leave
- [ ] Don't give your home address until transaction complete
- [ ] Consider bringing a friend for large purchases

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Scam listings | Medium | Verify in person, never wire money |
| Stolen goods | Low | Avoid "too good" luxury items |
| Aggressive negotiations | Medium | Set your maximum before engaging |
| Safety concerns | Medium | Public meetups, daylight hours |
| Account bans | Medium | Don't appear as professional reseller |

## 2. Craigslist

### Platform Overview

Craigslist remains a goldmine for motivated sellers and estate sales, though it requires more patience and filtering than modern platforms. Its anonymity cuts both ways - sellers are often more motivated but fraud risk is higher.

### Target Demographics

- **Primary Sellers:** Budget-conscious families, non-tech users
- **Secondary Sellers:** Seniors, first-time sellers
- **Avoid:** Anyone asking for payment before meeting

### Advanced Search Strategies

#### Boolean Search Techniques

```plaintext
+(term) requires inclusion
-(term) requires exclusion
category:[number] specific category
```plaintext
#### High-Value Search Patterns

| Search Pattern | Success Rate | Category Strength |
|----------------|--------------|-------------------|
| "garage sale" + neighborhood names | 90% | All categories |
| "estate sale" | 85% | Antiques, quality furniture |
| "multi-family" | 80% | Mixed household |
| "everything must go" | 75% | Complete liquidation |
| "priced to sell" | 70% | Motivated sellers |
| "firm on price" | 65% | Flexible sellers |
| "moved/selling cheap" | 70% | Moving situations |

#### Strategic Time-Based Search

- **Early bird (5-7 AM):** Fresh listings, less competition
- **Morning (7-10 AM):** Active sellers, quick responses
- **Midday:** Competitive period, act fast
- **Afternoon:** Less competition but fewer new listings
- **Evening:** Good for last-minute deals

### CL-Specific Tactics

#### Flagging Strategy

Many good deals get buried by aggregator bots. Learn to:
- [ ] Filter by "posting date" newest first
- [ ] Use "byseller" option when available
- [ ] Report suspicious/scraper posts
- [ ] Refresh frequently during peak hours

#### Email Response Tactics

Subject lines that get opens:
```
"Interested in [ITEM] - local pickup today"
"Quick question about your [ITEM]"
"Can we arrange pickup tomorrow?"
```

Email body template:
```
Hi [NAME],

Saw your listing for [ITEM] on Craigslist.
I'm a local buyer and can pick up today
with cash.

Is it still available?

Thanks,
[YOUR NAME]
```

### Safety Protocols

Craigslist has higher anonymity risk. Implement:
- [ ] Use proxy email (creates temporary address)
- [ ] Meet only in public places
- [ ] Never send deposits or wire money
- [ ] Inspect thoroughly before paying
- [ ] Consider using a disposable phone number
- [ ] Bring a friend for high-value pickups
- [ ] Document all communication

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Scam listings | High | Cash only, in-person inspection |
| Spam/bot listings | High | Filter by photo count, fresh posts |
| Stolen goods | Low-Medium | Avoid suspiciously cheap luxury |
| Personal safety | Medium | Public meetups, daylight |
| Phone number exposure | High | Use CL anonymous email |

## 3. OfferUp / LetGo

### Platform Overview

OfferUp (which merged with LetGo) is the mobile-first marketplace designed for casual sellers. The app-centric design and built-in rating system create a relatively safe environment, though deal quality varies by market.

### Target Demographics

- **Primary Sellers:** Families, young adults, casual declutterers
- **Secondary Sellers:** Small estate sales, apartment moves
- **Strength:** Clean interface, built-in messaging

### Advanced Search Strategies

#### App-Specific Features

- [ ] **Location radius:** Set 5-15 miles for best results
- [ ] **Make an Offer filter:** Find negotiation-friendly sellers
- [ ] **Urgent section:** Find price drops and motivated sellers
- [ ] **Saved searches:** Get notified of new matches
- [ ] **Similar listings:** Research competition

#### High-Value Keywords in OfferUp

| Search Term | Availability | Price Range |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|
| "bundle" | Medium | Variable |
| "or best offer" | High | Below market |
| "urgent" | High | Below market |
| "will negotiate" | High | Flexible |
| "need gone" | Medium | Low |
| "gift for someone" | Low | Variable |

#### Timing Strategy

- **App notifications:** Enable for saved searches
- **Best response window:** 9 AM - 12 PM
- **Weekend mornings:** Most listings appear
- **Evening deals:** 6-9 PM price flexibility

### OfferUp-Specific Features

#### Making Offers

- [ ] Start 20-30% below asking
- [ ] Use "make offer" button, not general message
- [ ] Include pickup timeline in offer
- [ ] Offer cash for faster response
- [ ] Reference other similar listings if pricing is firm

#### Communication Best Practices

```
"Hi! I can pick this up today and pay cash.
What's your best price for a quick sale?"

+ [Include photo of payment ready]

"Perfect, I'll be there within the hour!"
```

### Safety Features Built Into OfferUp

- [ ] Seller ratings visible to all users
- [ ] In-app messaging (no phone exchange needed)
- [ ] Report/block functionality
- [ ] Meetup location suggestions (public places)
- [ ] Tracked transactions for dispute resolution

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Low-ball offers from resellers | High | Ignore, focus on sellers |
| Counterfeit items | Low-Medium | Photo verification, ratings |
| Non-responsive sellers | Medium | Move on quickly |
| Account safety | Low | Platform handles most issues |

## 4. Estate Sales

### Platform Overview

Estate sales offer the highest quality merchandise at the best prices, but require more effort and upfront research. They're liquidation events for entire households, often including antiques, collectibles, and quality furniture.

### Target Demographics

- **Primary Sellers:** Estate executors, professional estate sale companies
- **Quality Level:** Often excellent, full-service households
- **Potential:** Can source entire inventory for weeks

### Finding Estate Sales

#### Primary Directories

- [ ] **EstateSales.NET** - largest directory
- [ ] **EstateSales.org** - comprehensive listings
- [ ] **Gottasellit.com** - regional strength varies
- [ ] **RubyLane.com** - higher-end focus
- [ ] **Facebook Groups** - local estate sale groups

#### Advance Research Checklist

Before attending any estate sale:
- [ ] Research the neighborhood (indicates quality)
- [ ] Check photos if available
- [ ] Note pricing strategy (tagged, negotiable, etc.)
- [ ] Identify sale company (reputation matters)
- [ ] Look for "preview" events
- [ ] Note any "second day" discount promises

### Estate Sale Day Strategies

#### Pre-Sale (if allowed)
- [ ] Arrive 30 minutes early if preview available
- [ ] Walk entire home before buying
- [ ] Ask staff about specific items
- [ ] Request "first dibs" on hold items
- [ ] Get staff contact for notifications

#### First Day Strategy
- [ ] Arrive 15 minutes before doors open
- [ ] Get on mailing list for future sales
- [ ] Focus on highest-value, smallest items
- [ ] Build rapport with estate sale company
- [ ] Don't Haggle on first day (prices firm)

#### Last Day Strategy
- [ ] Look for 50-75% discount items
- [ ] Focus on practical household items
- [ ] Electronics often deeply discounted
- [ ] Books, media, kitchen items common
- [ ] Be prepared to haul quickly

### Negotiation at Estate Sales

| Day | Strategy | Success Rate |
|-----|----------|--------------|
| Day 1 | Buy at full price | Best selection |
| Day 2 | 20-30% off standard | Good selection |
| Day 3 | 50%+ off standard | Limited selection |
| Final | 75% off, bundle deals | Very limited |

#### Opening Lines
- "I'm interested in everything in this room"
- "Do you have authority to negotiate on [item]?"
- "What will you take for all of these books?"
- "Is this the best price on the furniture?"

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Overbidding competition | High | Set maximum before entering |
| Quality vs. price mismatch | Medium | Research before buying |
| Transportation logistics | High | Plan vehicle, helpers |
| Timing conflicts | Medium | Multiple sales same day |
| Estate sale company reliability | Low | Check reviews online |

|------------|-------------|---------|----------------|
| Goodwill | Low-Medium | Variable | Electronics section |
| Savers | Medium | Good | Seasonal sections |

| Deseret Industries | Low | Good | LDS communities |
| St. Vincent de Paul | Low | Good | Regional strength |
| Local Hospice Thrift | Variable | Good | Unique finds |

### Store-Specific Strategies

#### Goodwill Strategies
- [ ] Visit during senior discount days (often Tuesday)
- [ ] Check "50% off color tag" days
- [ ] Electronics section = hidden gems
- [ ] Build staff relationships
- [ ] Check warehouse/bulk sections
- [ ] Note restocking days (often Tuesday/Thursday)

#### Savers Strategies
- [ ] First 50% off color tag days
- [ ] Senior discount days
- [ ] "Buy the Pink" breast cancer fundraiser days
- [ ] Larger format stores have better selection
- [ ] Check back frequently (high turnover)

#### Consignment Shop Strategies
- [ ] Research each store's focus (clothing, furniture, toys)
- [ ] Build relationship with owner
- [ ] Ask about "unclaimed items" policies
- [ ] Watch for seasonal clearance events
- [ ] Some accept consignment drops by appointment

## 6. Wholesale Liquidators

### Platform Overview

Wholesale liquidators offer bulk purchasing at 30-70% below retail. Success requires understanding lot sizes, quality variations, and return policies. Best for scaling operations beyond casual sourcing.

### Types of Liquidators

#### Direct Liquidators
- [ ] **DirectLiquidation.com** - returns friendly
- [ ] ** liquidation.com** - large lots
- [ ] **B-Stock Solutions** - retailer direct
- [ ] **GovDeals** - government surplus

#### Regional Liquidators
- [ ] Find local through business directories
- [ ] Attend trade shows
- [ ] Network through business associations
- [ ] Check warehouse districts

### Lot Evaluation Framework

#### Before Bidding

- [ ] Research retail prices on all items
- [ ] Calculate average selling price
- [ ] Verify authenticity for all electronics
- [ ] Check manifest accuracy (often 85-90%)
- [ ] Understand return policies
- [ ] Factor in shipping/transport costs

#### Manifest Analysis Template

```
Total Items in Lot: _____
Estimated Retail Value: $_____
Avg. Resale Value (60%): $_____
Lot Purchase Price: $_____
Platform Fees (15%): $_____
Shipping/Handling: $_____
Misc Costs: $_____
Total Costs: $_____
Profit Potential: $_____
Margin %: _____
```

### Minimum Standards for Lots

| Metric | Minimum Acceptable | Target |
|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Manifest Accuracy | 85% | 95%+ |
| Damaged Items | <10% | <5% |
| Resale Value Recovery | 50% | 70%+ |
| Time to List | <2 weeks | <1 week |
| Storage Duration | <30 days | <14 days |

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Unknown lot contents | High | Request manifests, buy samples |
| Condition variation | High | Assume 10% damaged |
| Price competition | High | Set maximum bid limits |
| Storage requirements | High | Plan before bidding |
| Returns process | Variable | Read policies carefully |

## 7. Auctions

### Platform Overview

Auctions offer excellent deals on bulk items, estate contents, and specialty collections. Success requires understanding bid strategies, condition assessment, and pickup logistics.

### Auction Types

| Auction Type | Deal Quality | Competition | Best For |
|--------------|--------------|-------------|----------|
| Local Estate | Very High | Medium | Furniture, antiques |
| Online | Medium | High | Electronics, bulk lots |
| Government | High | Medium | Mixed lots |
| Auto | Low | Varies | Parts only |
| Storage Unit | Medium | High | Mystery lots |

### Auction Platforms

#### Online Auctions
- [ ] **eBay Auctions** - baseline pricing reference
- [ ] **AuctionTime** - equipment focus
- [ ] **Proxibid** - heavy equipment
- [ ] **HiBid** - regional auctions
- [ ] **Facebook Live Auctions** - growing segment

#### Local Auctions
- [ ] **EstateSales.NET** - estate auctions
- [ ] ** local auction houses** - find regionally
- [ ] **County sheriff sales** - legal liens
- [ ] **Bank REO auctions** - foreclosed properties

### Bidding Strategies

#### Pre-Auction Research

- [ ] Preview all items in person
- [ ] Research comparable sold prices
- [ ] Calculate all-in costs including buyer's premium
- [ ] Set maximum bid for each item
- [ ] Identify your "hero items" (must-win)
- [ ] Identify your "pass items" (not worth it)

#### Day-of Auction

- [ ] Arrive 30 minutes early
- [ ] Get bidder number first
- [ ] Locate your target items
- [ ] Identify competition
- [ ] Watch for underbidding opportunities
- [ ] Stick to your maximums

#### Winning Bid Tactics

| Situation | Strategy | Example |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| Few bidders | Bid early, end early | Aggressive first bid |
| Many bidders | Wait, observe, strike | Let others fight |
| Your hero item | Set max, use proxy | $50 max bid |
| Overextended | Walk away | "Next lot" |
| Unknown value | Pass | No research = no bid |

### Auction Buyer's Premium

Most auctions charge a buyer's premium (BP) on top of winning bid:

```
Final Bid: $100
Buyer's Premium (15%): $15
Total: $115
+ Applicable taxes
```

**Always factor BP into your max bid calculation.**

### Risk Assessment

| Risk Factor | Level | Mitigation |
|-------------|-------|------------|
| Overbidding | High | Set maximums before auction |
| Condition surprises | Medium | Preview thoroughly |
| Competition tactics | Medium | Don't react emotionally |
| Pickup logistics | High | Arrange before bidding |
| Payment methods | Medium | Bring multiple payment options |
